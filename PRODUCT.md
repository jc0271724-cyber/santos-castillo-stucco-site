# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Houston-area homeowners (with a secondary commercial audience) evaluating
contractors for stucco, natural stone, exterior painting, and full remodeling.
They are comparison-shopping — often on a phone, frequently bilingual — and are
weighing trust and proof of quality before they invite anyone to their home.
The audience is bilingual English/Spanish, and both languages are first-class.

## Product Purpose

Turn a visitor into a booked estimate. The site does two jobs equally: prove
the quality of the work through real finished-project photography, and make
requesting a free estimate or callback effortless from any point in the page.
Success is a qualified homeowner who has seen the craft and tapped "get an
estimate" — in either language.

## Brand Personality

Premium craftsmanship. Three words: **precise, established, refined.** The voice
is the confident master builder, not the discount handyman and not a flashy
startup. It earns trust by showing finished work and speaking plainly about
process, price, and warranty. Warmth comes from the materials and the work, not
from hype.

## Anti-references

- Generic dark-navy + gold "luxury SaaS" templates (what the current site leans
  toward) — reads as AI-generated, not as a real craftsman.
- Cream / beige / parchment "editorial" AI aesthetic — the other saturated default.
- Commodity lead-gen directories (Angi / HomeAdvisor clutter, stock "handyman"
  photography, badge soup).
- Loud gradient buttons, side-stripe accent cards, and "01 / 02 / 03" numbered
  section scaffolding — template tells, not brand voice.

## Design Principles

- **Show the craft.** Real project photography outranks adjectives. A finished
  wall sells better than the word "exceptional."
- **Material honesty.** Palette, texture, and type echo the actual trade —
  stucco, plaster, stone, lime — rather than borrowing a tech-brand look.
- **The next step is always one tap away.** Estimate / callback is reachable
  from every fold; the portfolio funnels into it.
- **Bilingual parity.** English and Spanish get equal weight, equal polish,
  equal layout integrity — no afterthought translations.
- **Quiet confidence.** Premium is restraint and precision, not gradients and
  motion for its own sake.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Target WCAG 2.1 AA: body text ≥ 4.5:1 contrast, large text ≥ 3:1, visible
  focus states, real labels on every form control.
- Full bilingual (EN/ES) support with correct `lang` handling.
- Honor `prefers-reduced-motion` for every animation.
- Mobile-first: the page must hold up on a phone in daylight, since that is
  where most homeowners will first see it.
