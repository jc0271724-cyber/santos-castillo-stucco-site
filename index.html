Our Story

Built on Precision.
Trusted by Houston's Finest.

S. Castillo Construction has spent over two decades perfecting the art of exterior finishing for Houston's most distinguished homes and commercial properties. What began as a family trade has grown into one of the region's most respected names in stucco, stone, and architectural remodeling.

We work exclusively with premium materials and employ craftsmen who treat every surface as a signature. Our clients trust us with their most significant investment — and we take that responsibility seriously.

From intimate residential renovations to large-scale commercial facades, every project receives the same meticulous attention that has defined our reputation across Pasadena, South Houston, and the greater Houston metro.
