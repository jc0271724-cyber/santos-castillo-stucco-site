Our Story

Built on Precision.
Trusted by Houston's Finest.

S. Castillo Construction has spent over two decades perfecting the art of exterior finishing for Houston's most distinguished homes and commercial properties. What began as a family trade has grown into one of the region's most respected names in stucco, stone, and architectural remodeling.

We work exclusively with premium materials and employ craftsmen who treat every surface as a signature. Our clients trust us with their most significant investment — and we take that responsibility seriously.

From intimate residential renovations to large-scale commercial facades, every project receives the same meticulous attention that has defined our reputation across Pasadena, South Houston, and the greater Houston metro.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does stucco cost per square foot?

▼

Stucco costs vary based on the type of finish, surface condition, and project size. We provide free on-site estimates with transparent pricing — no hidden fees. Call us at 832-781-9726 for a personalized quote.

How long does a stucco project take?

▼

A typical residential stucco project takes 1-3 weeks depending on size and complexity. Commercial projects may take longer. We provide a detailed timeline with every estimate and keep you updated at every stage.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

▼

Yes. We stand behind every project we complete. Our work is backed by our craftsmanship guarantee, and the materials we use come with manufacturer warranties. Your investment is protected.

How long does stucco last in Houston's climate?

▼

Properly applied stucco can last 50+ years in Houston's climate. The key is correct application technique and quality materials — both of which are our specialty. We also use moisture barriers and proper drainage details to protect against humidity.

Do you work on both residential and commercial?

▼

Absolutely. We handle everything from single-family homes to large commercial properties like hotels and retail centers. Our team is experienced, licensed, and insured for projects of any scale across the greater Houston area.

Are your estimates really free?

▼

100% free, no obligation. We visit your property, assess the scope, and provide a detailed written estimate with materials, labor, and timeline. There's never any pressure — we let our work speak for itself.
