Our Story

Built on Precision. Trusted by Houston's Finest.

S. Castillo Construction has spent over two decades perfecting the art of exterior finishing for Houston's most distinguished homes and commercial properties — one of the region's most respected names in stucco, stone, and architectural remodeling.

We work exclusively with premium materials and employ craftsmen who treat every surface as a signature. From intimate renovations to large-scale commercial facades, every project receives the same meticulous attention.

Yes. Our work is backed by our craftsmanship guarantee, and materials come with manufacturer warranties. Your investment is protected.

Properly applied stucco lasts 50+ years here. The key is correct technique and quality materials — our specialty — plus moisture barriers for humidity.
